# betterwithai Marketing & Content Style Guide

**Strategy Overview (from X research + vision):**
- Content flywheel is non-negotiable for 60%+ organic traffic.
- Personal brands + EGC (employee generated content).
- Video for trust (product demos, customer stories, lifestyle).
- Lead magnets (AI Freedom Audit) -> wizard -> self-serve Roadmap.
- Hybrid: Self-serve for volume + warm outbound/content for expansion.
- Post daily on X/LinkedIn. Threads + Reels primary.
- SEO/AEO: Show up in AI search (Reddit, reviews, frameworks).

**Content Pillars:**
1. Tactical Value: Frameworks, "7 questions", exact steps, templates.
2. Lifestyle Proof: "AI runs business while I travel/family time" - raw/fun Holderness style.
3. Results Stories: Client wins, before/after (anonymized or with permission).
4. Hype Roasts: Fun takedowns of 50-tool overwhelm, jargon.
5. Behind the Curtain: How agents/systems work (transparency builds trust).

**Format Guidelines:**
- **X Threads:** 8-12 tweets. Hook first. Numbered. Tactical in middle. Strong CTA to wizard at end. Emojis sparingly for scannability.
- **Reels/Shorts:** 15-60s. Face + text overlay. Lifestyle + one tip + link in bio.
- **LinkedIn:** Longer posts, carousels, lead magnet CTAs ("Comment X for template").
- **Email:** Value-first, short, direct CTA. 48h activation focus.
- **Lead Magnets:** Free audit/score -> email capture -> nurture to $1497 Roadmap.

**Voice in Content:**
- Short sentences. One idea per line/paragraph.
- Humor: Self-aware, hype-roasting.
- End with action: "Start here: betterwithai.io/#wizard"
- Raw + polished: Show real life, deliver premium value.

**Posting Cadence (Autonomous):**
- 1-2 X threads/week per brand voice.
- Daily short form.
- Repurpose winners to Reels, LinkedIn, newsletter.
- Track: Wizard starts per post. Kill low performers.

**Branding in Marketing:**
- Always link to self-serve.
- Use light premium visuals.
- Test hooks from ContentMachine (e.g. "The 7 questions...", lifestyle freedom).
- Consistent: $1,497 Roadmap as hero.

**Measurement:** UTM on all. Wizard conversion, 48h activation, LTV from content cohort.
