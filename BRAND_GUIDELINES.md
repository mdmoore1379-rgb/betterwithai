# betterwithai Brand Guidelines

**Overall Aesthetic:** Light, modern, premium, clean. Tesla-simple. High-end consulting feel with approachable humor. Pure light theme. Maximum readability and contrast.

**Color Palette:**
- Background: #FFFFFF (pure white)
- Text: #0F0F0F or #111 (near black for high contrast)
- Accent: #0A66C2 (professional blue)
- Supporting: Subtle grays like #E5E5E3 for borders/tracks, green-200+ for success badges with dark text.
- Buttons/CTAs: Solid dark text on light or accent blue. Explicit borders (e.g. border-[#0F0F0F]).

**Typography:** Geist Sans (primary), Geist Mono (code), Space Grotesk (headings). Clean, modern, highly legible.

**Logo & Icon:** Clean modern wordmark "betterwithai". Minimal abstract icon (line + dot style). No cartoon mascots in hero, nav, or primary visuals. Mascot (if any) only in supporting playful spots.

**Visual Rules (High-Contrast Mandate):**
- EVERY element readable: No white text on light, no dark on dark.
- Solid badge backgrounds (e.g. green-200 with dark text).
- Explicit borders and text colors on buttons/links.
- Progress tracks: light like #E5E5E3.
- Images: High quality, lifestyle (travel/family showing freedom AI enables), clean mockups. No low-contrast overlays.
- Layout: Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, mobile-first.

**Brand Voice (Detailed):**
- **Core:** Irreverent + serious results. "We take the work dead seriously. We just don't take ourselves seriously."
- **Inspiration:** Holderness family (raw/fun travel/lifestyle + AI doing the work so you live more). Liquid Death irreverence + Dr. Squatch bold humor targeted at AI overwhelm.
- **Do:** Short punchy lines. Roast hype/jargon. Show real freedom (trips, family, time back). Emotional hooks + tactical value. Confident swagger. "Buy back your time."
- **Don't:** Corporate speak, condescension, crude jokes at customer expense, low-value fluff, black/dark themes.
- **Positioning Hook:** "Everyone's talking about AI. Nobody tells you what to *do* with it." Then deliver clear plan + execution.

**Marketing/Branding Applications:**
- Hero: Lifestyle + emotional (freedom) + immediate value (wizard CTA).
- Content: Raw stories + frameworks. End with direct self-serve link.
- Portal/Products: Premium clean, high contrast, progress visible, no friction.
- Social: Personal brand heavy, video of real life + AI wins.

Update all visuals/code to match. Audit for contrast. This is non-negotiable for premium $100M positioning.
