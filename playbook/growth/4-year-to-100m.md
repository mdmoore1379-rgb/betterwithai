# betterwithai — Path to $100M Revenue by End of 2030

**Starting Point:** Jan 2027 (full-time focus)  
**Target:** $100M ARR by December 2030 (4 years)  
**Owner:** Michael Moore  
**Core Belief:** AI is the ultimate business superpower for making better choices. We productize the "how" at scale.

## Revenue Ramp (Aggressive but Achievable with Productization + Leverage)

- **2027 (Year 1 - Foundation & Productization):** $2M - $5M ARR  
  Focus: Nail self-serve Roadmap, build initial team (3-5), deliver high-profile projects, content flywheel.  
  Key: 70% services (projects/retainers), 30% product (Roadmaps + early digital).

- **2028 (Year 2 - Team & Systems):** $10M - $15M ARR  
  Scale delivery team, launch AI Ops Platform (internal tools productized), Academy (training). Hire first sales/enablement.  
  Mix: 50% services, 30% product/platform, 20% education/partnerships.

- **2029 (Year 3 - Scale & Channels):** $30M - $40M ARR  
  Enterprise sales motion, channel partnerships (MSPs, consultants), international. Multiple product lines.  
  Team: 50-80 people. Heavy automation via agents.

- **2030 (Year 4 - Hypergrowth):** $100M ARR  
  Platform becomes core (recurring revenue), large enterprise deals, M&A or JV for distribution, global brand.  
  Mix: 30% services, 50% product/platform/recurring, 20% ecosystem (training, partners, IP).

**Key Levers to $100M (not headcount linear):**
- Productization: Fixed offers, templates, software (Roadmap as entry, retainers as expansion).
- Leverage: Agents + lean team (RevenueEngine + ContentMachine + AcquisitionFlywheel doing 80%).
- Hybrid motion: Strong self-serve wizard for volume + warm outbound + light sales for bigger ACV (per research: self-serve hard in pure B2B, layer sales fast).
- Content flywheel: Personal brands + EGC + video + lead magnets (non-negotiable).
- 48h onboarding as core retention moat.
- Pricing tests early and often. Revenue first, margins later.
- Warm outbound on engaged leads (site visitors, content engagers, partial wizards).

**New 2026-2027 focus (from research + agent cycles):**
- Nail $1,497 self-serve Roadmap as the hero productized entry.
- 48-hour activation system in portal.
- LaunchOperator running phased public launch with content + warm outbound.
- Financial model live for cash and hiring triggers.
- Pricing power + volume.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Offer Architecture (Evolve from Brief)
- **Tier 1: AI Roadmap** — Flagship self-serve entry ($X one-time, high volume).
- **Tier 2: AI Projects** — Done-for-you implementations (scalable via standardized playbooks + delivery pods).
- **Tier 3: AI Retainers / Platform** — Recurring AI operating system (high LTV).
- **New: AI Academy + Certification** (2028).
- **New: betterwithai Platform** (internal tools → product, 2028-29).
- **IP & Licensing:** Frameworks, prompt libraries, case study data.

### 2. Go-to-Market Evolution
- 2027: Direct + content + referrals (from M3 network).
- 2028: Partnerships (MSPs, VCs, software cos).
- 2029+: Enterprise outbound + product-led growth.
- Brand: Bold, irreverent, results-obsessed (Liquid Death energy).

### 3. Operations at Scale (Agent-Powered)
- Ops Leader Agent orchestrates all specialists.
- Automate: Proposals, onboarding, project tracking, billing, recruiting screening.
- Delivery: Standardized pods (1 lead + agents + humans).
- Metrics dashboard (build in 2027).

### 4. Team & Org Build
- 2027: Michael + 2-3 delivery + 1 ops.
- 2028: Add sales, marketing, product roles. First managers.
- 2029: Functional VPs.
- 2030: 100+ people, possible multiple entities.

**Hiring Philosophy:** Hire for character + leverage. Use agents for screening.

### 5. Financial Discipline
- High gross margins (70%+ on products, 50%+ on services initially).
- Reinvest 30-40% into growth (marketing, product, talent).
- Bootstrap or strategic capital only.
- Track: CAC:LTV, Magic Number, NRR, Rule of 40.

### 6. Risk Mitigation & Milestones
**2027 Milestones (prove model):**
- 50+ Roadmap sales.
- 10 flagship projects/retainers.
- Content engine live (weekly).
- Agent system handling 60%+ ops tasks.
- First 5-person team.

**2028:** Product launch, $10M run rate, repeatable sales process.

**2029:** Platform beta, partnerships driving 30% revenue.

**2030:** $100M, profitable at scale, category leader.

**Kill Switches:** If NRR <100% or CAC payback >18mo after Year 2, pivot.
